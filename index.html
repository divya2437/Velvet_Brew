Our Story

A Corner for the Soul

Velvet Brew was born from a simple belief — that a great cup of coffee deserves unhurried attention. We source single-origin beans, brew every cup by hand, and design every corner of our space to make you feel at home.

Whether you're journaling over a latte, catching up with an old friend, or simply watching the rain — this is your place.
